Desktop Wallet

Part of my work at Musicoin is heading The Musicoin Bounty Program which exists to incubate innovative ideas that come up within the community and to fund research and developmen related to the Music industry. One such initiative is the Musicoin Desktop Wallet.

The Musicoin Desktop Wallet is written using the node-webkit framework. Its primary goal is to be a minimalistic, user friendly wallet that enables easy transfer of funds to and from other wallets.

I co-maintain the Musicoin Wallet, implementing new features, signing new releases, opening new issues, suggesting improvements and reporting new bugs. I also assign bounties to issues to invite people to contribute to open source development. The Musicoin community is amazing and I love every minute of being a small part of the amazing movement.
