Npcap

Npcap is a packet capture framework part of the nmap suite. Its function is similar to tshark in that it is used to capture network traffic which can be analysed with the help of a Wireshark like UI.

Npcap was in its nascent stages when I started working on it, so it was buggy and had lots of cool issues to tackle and fix. I reported bugs, filed issues and helped the lead maintainer fix and issue patches for them. I also helped reproduce some issues so that we could log waht was happening and tackle them later.

Npcap was fun in other ways as well. I could analyse traffic that was stemming from the Open WiFi router back at school and find interesting statistics. Most of all, Npcap worked perfectly with nmap, so I could do tests on remote servers from Shodan to find and report vulnerabilities.
