Npcap

Npcap is a packet capture framework that is part of the nmap suite. Its primary function is similar to that of tshark in that its used to capture network traffic which can then be analysed with the help of an UI like Wireshark.

Npcap was in its nascent stages when I started working on it, so it was buggy and had lots of problems. Being an Open Source project, I reported bugs and helped the lead maintainer fix commonly known issues and issue patches for them. I also helped reproduce known bugs so we could log them in the appropriate forum.

Npcap was also fun in that I could analyse traffic that was stemming from my Open WiFi router back at school and I could find out interesting statistics such as the most visited sites and most clicked on advertisements. Most of all, Npcap worked perfectly with nmap, so I could do some interesting tests on remote servers which one can get from Shodan.
