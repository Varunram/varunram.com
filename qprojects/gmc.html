Go-Musicoin

The Musicoin project is somewhat close to my heart since I have been there since the very founding of the coin. The main idea of the coin when we started it was simple - remove the middlemen in the Muisc industry that usurp honest musicians' rewards the same way bitcoin removed financial intermediaries. It started out pretty simple with 2 people working on the project. Now our team is over 15 members and continues to grow strong.

Musicoin was forked off from Ethereum in order to avoid doing the same kind of thigns that Ethereum protocol developers had already done. We chose go-musicoin since that was the most popular Ethereum client implementation out there and had lots of dev support.

In the initial stages, I did not actively work on go-musicoin since there weren't many protocol changes that had to be implemented. But I gradually stepped in more since some team members left the project and it was up to me to implement the protocol changes to take the project forward.

My contributions to go-musicoin are mostly testing for consensus failures, making sure everything runs fine and general bug fixes. I also implement any new Musicoin Improvement Proposals that are proposed by the members of the community.
