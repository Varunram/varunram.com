BIPs

Bitcoin is the world's first popularly adopted cryptocurrency and the pioneer of the blockchain reviolution. Bitcoin Improvement Proposals / BIPs are the standard way for one to suggest changes in the Bitocin protocol to get them to be implemented by Bitcoin clients.

My work with BIPs are mostly correcting typographical errors that may alter the meaning of a particular improvement proposal. While not much, this has made me read a lot of BIPs in the process and has given invaluable knowledge in the process. An advantage that one gets is the ability to interact with some of the best open soruce developers in the world and get to ask doubts and share ideas.
