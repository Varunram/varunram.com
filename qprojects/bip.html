BIPs

Bitcoin is the world's first popularly adopted cryptocurrency and the pioneer of the blockchain revolution. Bitcoin Improvement Proposals / BIPs are the standard way for one to suggest changes to the Bitcoin protocol.

My contribution to BIPs are mostly correcting typographical / math errors that may alter the meaning of a particular proposal. Though not much, this has led to me reading a lot of BIPs and given invaluable knowledge in the process. Another advantage is the ability to interact with some of the best open source developers in the world and get to ask questions and share ideas.
