LIT

Lit is the Bitcoin Lightning Network implementation at the MIT Media Lab. The Lightning Network is a Layer 2 scaling solution for Bitcoin which enables low fees, instant payments without requiring third party custody Lit is written primarily in Go with a frontend UI written in Javascript.

Lit is different from the other projects I've worked on due to it being a research project taking pilot initiatives to implement something new on the protocol level. Proposing a change is not as simple as just having code but has performance, optimization and usefulness dimensions as well. In most cases, there is no reference implementation to refer since changes are mostly new and hence the process involves extensive experimentation.

Lit has many passionate people working on it and the opportunity to interact with them virtually and in person is a privilege. During my time at the Lab, I worked on improving spam detection, researched into novel Proof of Stake algorithms, bug fixes for lit among others. I also interacted with people from other projects and got to see so many new and pathberaking things being developed.
