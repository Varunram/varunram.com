Frrole.AI

Frrole.AI is a company which does Profile Assessment for Recruiters and HR Managers. The product I worked on called DeepSense, was one which accumulated data from different sources and provides personality insights based on four categories - Extroversion, Emotional Stability, Agreeableness and Conscientiousness. A DISC score is also provided, making it easy for recruiters to know which which attributes a target user possesses more.

I worked on systems at Frrole, setting up their backend servers to deal with the data pipeline that already existed and also worked on implementing image based analysis using data from Instagram to Frrole's Data Pipeline. Along with this, I incorporated a third party service called ClearBit as a replacement for an existing data provider.

Frrole was an entirely new experience for me since I learnt new stuff that were not even closely related to my domain of networks. I chose to work with Frrole because I found their idea interesting and wanted to do imeplement something productive towards the end. This also gave me the chance to learn more about machine learning models, which is something I've always wanted to do.
