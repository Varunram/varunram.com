Awesome-Regex

Initially started off as my personal compilation list, awesome regex resources are a compilation of the best regex resources available on the internet. They are classified based on their application and contains tons of cool resources for all levels.

The story behind awesome regex is quite interesting - I developed my own list named similarly and only after publishing did I come across the main list. We decided to merge both lists and make a single more awesome list, which continues to be maintained today

Awesome regex is mostly focused on providing people a list of resources that they can choose from instead of writing a traditional guide. This gives learners more flexibility, something that I found to be lacking in the regex world which was more focused on guides and tutorials
