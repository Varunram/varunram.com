Awesome-Regex

Initially starting off as a personal list, awesome regex resources is a compilation of the best regex resources available on the internet. Resources are classified on the basis of their application and the collection contains tons of such resources for all difficulty levels.

The story behind awesome regex is quite interesting - I developed my own list named similarly and only after publishing did I come across another one. In the end, the maintainer and I decided to merge both lists and make a single more awesome list, which continues to be maintained today.

Awesome regex is focused on providing people with a list of resources that they can choose from instead of proceeding with writing a traditional guide. This gives learners the flexibility of choice, something I peronally found to be absent in the regex world which happened to focus more on guides and tutorials to teach beginners.
