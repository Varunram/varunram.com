Dangerous Dave

Dangerous Dave is a remake of the classic retro game Dave in C++.

It stands as one of my favourite projects since I got to learn lots of new things while still working on the stuff that I like. Retro games have alwyas been a passion of mine and it was ncie to finally see the dream realized in the form of a game that I made from scratch myself. It was also the first large scale project that I undertook.
