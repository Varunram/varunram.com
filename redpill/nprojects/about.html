Varunram Ganesh is a venture capitalist and student at Duke University's Fuqua School of Business

His background is in technology research, data privacy and product strategy, and his interests span cryptocurrencies, personal data security and emerging technologies.

As a venture Capitalist, Varunram focuses on emerging technologies and looks to test new products, identify interesting founders, and enable them to create something different.

As an active entrepreneur, he performs research on the intersection of data privacy and student inclusion at Kratos, a startup focused on student data privacy, and leads Bitcoin research initiatives at BitHyve.

In the past, Varunram was part of the MIT Digital Currency Initiative, the Yale Open Innovation Lab, the founding team at Musicoin, and worked on the Bitcoin Lightning Network.

In his spare time, he loves to play arcade games and watch horror movies.
