Varunram Ganesh is a macro fixed income and equities investor.

His background is in venture capital, technology research, and product strategy, and his interests span public markets, cryptocurrencies, and emerging technologies.

As an investor, he believes in investing with high conviction and relies on the power of societal transitions and events as opposed to quantitiative value metrics.

As a startup enthusiast, he likes testing products, giving feedback and helping connect people with similar interests.

In the past, Varunram was part of the MIT Digital Currency Initiative, the Yale Open Innovation Lab, the founding team at Musicoin, and worked on the Bitcoin Lightning Network.

In his spare time, he loves to play arcade games and watch horror movies.
